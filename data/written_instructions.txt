Hi there! Thank you so much for trying our storytelling game! Hearing the stories you tell today will help us improve this game and find out if it is helpful!

Here is how it works:
In each story, you will see pictures, one at a time. Look at everything in the picture. When you are ready to speak, hold the Space key and tell the story of what is happening! Try to describe the characters, where they are, what they do, how they feel, and what they think or say. There are no wrong answers.

After a little while, a "Next" button will appear. You can press it to go to the next picture, or keep going if you have more to say!
Sometimes, the pictures might move. Sometimes, they won't. Both are normal.

First, you will try two short practice stories to get used to the game. After that, you will come back to this screen.
Then, you will tell four longer stories. After each story, you will come back to this screen to pick the next one.
