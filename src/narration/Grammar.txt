META-PRINCIPLES FOR THE SYSTEM
Across all categories, a few physics principles recur:
1. The object resists the false claim — briefly attempts to become what was claimed, fails, snaps back to truth
2. The true property intensifies — becomes more saturated, more extreme, undeniable
3. Contrast is made visible — juxtaposition, comparison, before/after, either/or
4. Physics demonstrates truth — gravity, magnetism, force, resistance, flow all visualize the actual state
5. The body/object performs its truth — through behavior, not symbols
