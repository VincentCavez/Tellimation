Welcome to the Tellimation Study

Thank you for participating in this research study. You will watch short animations and answer questions about illustrated scenes.

The study consists of two parts:

  • Part 1 (~13 min): Watch animations and choose what you would say about each scene.
  • Part 2 (~7 min): Read descriptions and rate how appropriate they are.

Please complete the study in one sitting, in a quiet environment.
